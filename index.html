JUJUTSU KAISEN

WIKI

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime where cursed energy fuels intense battles, and sorcerers fight to protect humanity from monstrous curses.

JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes host to a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna. After swallowing Sukuna's finger to save his friends, Yuji joins Tokyo Jujutsu High to train as a sorcerer and consume all of Sukuna's fingers so the curse can be destroyed.

CURSED TECHNIQUES & POWERS

LIMITLESS

User: Satoru Gojo

The Limitless allows the user to manipulate space at will. It creates an infinite space between the user and their surroundings, making the user essentially untouchable. Combined with the Six Eyes, this technique makes Gojo the strongest sorcerer in the series.

Infinity

Creates an infinite space that slows down and stops attacks before they reach the user

Blue

Creates a vacuum that pulls everything toward a single point

Red

Repels and pushes away everything with explosive force

Purple

The convergence of Blue and Red, erasing anything it touches from existence

Ten Shadows Technique

User: Megumi Fushiguro

Allows the user to summon and control ten different shikigami, each with unique abilities.

Straw Doll Technique

User: Nobara Kugisaki

Inflicts damage on targets through voodoo-like resonance using nails and straw dolls.

Boogie Woogie

User: Aoi Todo

Instantly swaps the positions of two objects, including the user and others.

Ratio Technique

User: Kento Nanami

Creates a weak point on any target by dividing it into tenths.

Black Flash

Various Users

A critical hit that occurs when a sorcerer applies cursed energy within 0.000001 seconds of a physical hit.

Cursed Speech

User: Toge Inumaki

Words spoken with cursed energy become commands that targets are forced to obey.
