TECHNIQUES

In Jujutsu Kaisen, cursed techniques are innate supernatural abilities powered by cursed energy. From manipulating space to controlling shadows, these techniques form the core of a sorcerer's power.

INNATE TECHNIQUES

Innate techniques are abilities inherited through bloodlines or developed naturally by sorcerers. They form the base of a jujutsu sorcerer's fighting style.

Limitless

Satoru Gojo

The Limitless allows manipulation of space at an atomic level, creating an "infinity" between the user and their surroundings. When paired with the Six Eyes, this technique becomes nearly invincible.

Infinity Blue Red Purple

Ten Shadows Technique

Megumi Fushiguro

Allows the user to summon and control ten different shikigami. Each shikigami has unique abilities and can be combined for stronger effects.

Divine Dogs Nue Toad Max Elephant

Straw Doll Technique

Nobara Kugisaki

Using a hammer and nails, this technique creates resonance between a straw doll and target, allowing damage to be transferred through a voodoo-like effect.

Resonance Hairpin Straw Doll Nail

Blood Manipulation

Choso

Allows precise control over the user's blood, shaping it into weapons or projectiles and manipulating its properties.

Flowing Red Scale Piercing Blood Supernova

Boogie Woogie

Aoi Todo

Allows the user to instantly swap the positions of any two objects, including themselves, by clapping their hands together.

Position Swap Strategic Combat

Cursed Speech

Toge Inumaki

Words spoken with cursed energy become commands that the target is compelled to follow, though stronger targets can resist the effects.

Don't Move Blast Away Explode

Projection Sorcery

Naoya & Naobito Zenin

Divides a second into 24 frames. Allows the user to move at lightning-fast speeds by projecting actions frame-by-frame. Any object touched must follow this speed or freeze.

Divides a second into 24 frames High-speed movements Freezes

Idle Transfiugration

Mahito

Alters the shape of souls directly, allowing instant body manipulation. Can reshape his own body or kill others by disfiguring their souls. Extremely dangerous.

Soul Manipulation Self-Transfiguration Instant Death

Ratio Technique

Kento Nanami

Automatically targets a weak point on any object or enemy using a 7:3 ratio. Striking that point results in critical damage. Effective against both cursed spirits and humans.

Creates a 7:3 ratio weak point on targets Guaranteed critical hit Blunt sword

Star Rage/Virtual Mass Manipulation (Unofficial Name)

Yuki Tsukumo

Manipulates virtual mass to create gravity-based attacks. Can form black hole-like destruction through domain techniques.

Manipulates virtual mass Creates black hole-like destructive impact Immense power

Copy Technique

Yuta Okkotsu

Through his bond with Rika, Yuta can copy and use others’ innate techniques. Known copies: Cursed Speech, Reverse Cursed Technique, etc.

Cursed Speech (Toge's technique) Sky Manipulation / Star Rage (possibly) Reverse Cursed Technique Domain Expansion

Disaster Curse Techniques

Jogo, Hanami, Mahito, Dagon

The Disaster Curses are a group of powerful Special Grade Cursed Spirits born from the collective fear and hatred of natural disasters and environmental threats. Each of them represents a primal fear of humanity—like fire, water, forests, and the earth.

Jogo (Cursed Spirit - Volcano) Hanami (Cursed Spirit - Forest) Dagon (Cursed Spirit - Ocean) Mahito (listed above - Idle Transfiguration)

Construction Technique

Mai Zenin

Can construct one physical object out of nothing per day using cursed energy. Highly draining. She creates a real bullet in one iconic scene.

Constructs physical objects from cursed energy Limit: One per day due to energy drain Created real bullets or weapons out of thin air

Puppet Manipulation

Kokichi Muta

Can construct one physical object out of nothing per day using cursed energy. Highly draining. She creates a real bullet in one iconic scene.

Remote puppet Missiles, laser cannons, and armored plating Simple Domain

Sky Manipulation

Uro Takako (Culling Game)

Can construct one physical object out of nothing per day using cursed energy. Highly draining. She creates a real bullet in one iconic scene.

Manipulates space and sky Distorts direction
