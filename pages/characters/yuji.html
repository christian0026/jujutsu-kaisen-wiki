Background

Yuji Itadori was born to a normal family, raised by his grandfather after his parents' death. His grandfather, who was on his deathbed, imparted Yuji with a simple but profound lesson: "Help others, and die with no regrets." This influenced Yuji's sense of morality and his strong desire to protect others. He was an exceptional athlete and lived a relatively carefree life until one fateful day when he encountered a cursed object—Sukuna’s finger—at his school.

When Yuji's friends accidentally unsealed the cursed object, they were attacked by cursed spirits. To save them, Yuji consumed the finger, becoming the host of the malevolent Curse, Ryomen Sukuna. This act set him on the path to becoming a Jujutsu Sorcerer. Yuji was introduced to Jujutsu High, where he trained under Satoru Gojo and met other sorcerers like Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki. He learned that his mission was to collect all of Sukuna's fingers and ultimately eliminate the Curse, even if it meant sacrificing himself.

Personality

Yuji is kind-hearted, selfless, and driven by a strong sense of justice, largely shaped by his grandfather's final words. He genuinely cares about others and often goes to great lengths to protect people, even if it means putting himself in danger. Despite the chaos and violence he faces, Yuji remains optimistic and believes in the value of life, often seeing good in others and wanting to help them, regardless of the circumstances.

"I don’t want to regret not saving someone."

ABILITIES & POWERS

Yuji is one of Tokyo Jujutsu High’s strongest students, with Satoru Gojo ranking his potential alongside Yuta and Hakari. Despite his late start, he battles Grade 1 and Special-Grade curses using superhuman strength, cursed energy mastery, and the rare ability to perceive souls—even harming Mahito, who’s immune to conventional attacks.

Superhuman Strength & Speed

Yuji possesses immense physical prowess, allowing him to overpower curses and even rival special-grade sorcerers in close combat. His reflexes and agility are enhanced far beyond normal humans.

Divergent Fist

A unique fighting style where Yuji delays the impact of his punches, creating a double-hit effect that disrupts an opponent’s cursed energy flow.

Black Flash

A rare, high-risk technique that amplifies a strike’s power exponentially when cursed energy is applied within 0.000001 seconds of impact.

Cursed Energy Reinforcement

Yuji channels cursed energy to enhance his durability, speed, and attack power, making him a formidable hand-to-hand combatant.

Blood Manipulation (Temporary)

After consuming Death Painting Wombs, Yuji briefly gained Choso’s Blood Manipulation, allowing him to shape and weaponize his blood.

Sukuna’s Residual Influence

Though no longer a vessel, traces of Sukuna’s power may linger, granting Yuji heightened resistance to curses and potential unseen abilities.

RELATIONSHIPS

Sukuna

Their relationship is purely antagonistic, with Sukuna viewing Yuji as a temporary vessel and Yuji despising Sukuna for his cruelty. Though forced to coexist, Yuji constantly resists Sukuna’s influence, leading to tense internal and external conflicts. Sukuna occasionally taunts Yuji, making their dynamic volatile and unpredictable.

Gojo Satoru

Gojo acts as a mentor and father figure to Yuji, believing deeply in his potential as a sorcerer. Their relationship is lighthearted, with Gojo often joking around, but he also guides Yuji through the harsh realities of the jujutsu world. Yuji looks up to him, though he sometimes finds Gojo’s antics exhausting.

Choso

Initially enemies, Choso later recognizes Yuji as his "brother" due to their shared connection with the Death Paintings. Choso becomes fiercely protective of Yuji, even turning against Kenjaku for his sake. Yuji, though confused by Choso’s sudden loyalty, gradually accepts their strange familial bond.
