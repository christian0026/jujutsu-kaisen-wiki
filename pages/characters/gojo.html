Background

Satoru Gojo is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. He serves as a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he mentors main characters including Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, and Nobara Kugisaki.

Born into the prestigious Gojo family, one of the Three Big Jujutsu families, Satoru inherited the coveted Six Eyes and Limitless technique combination that appears only once every few hundred years. This rare inherited trait makes him virtually unbeatable in battle.

Personality

Gojo maintains a playful, carefree, and sometimes childish demeanor despite his immense power. He is extremely confident in his abilities, often to the point of arrogance, but his power legitimately backs up his claims. While he presents himself as aloof, he deeply cares for his students and is determined to change the corrupt jujutsu world for future generations.

"Throughout Heaven and Earth, I alone am the honored one."

ABILITIES & POWERS

Limitless Cursed Technique

Special Grade

The Limitless is Gojo's innate technique that allows him to manipulate space at the atomic level. With this, he can control the space between himself and anything else, essentially creating an infinite space.

Infinity

Gojo's primary defensive ability that creates an infinite space between himself and any incoming attack. It essentially makes him untouchable by slowing down approaching objects to a near stop before they can reach him.

Lapse: Blue

Creates a vacuum by amplifying the divergence of space, pulling everything toward a single point with immense force. This technique creates a gravitational pull so strong it can crush objects caught in its range.

Reversal: Red

The opposite of Blue, Red pushes everything away by amplifying the convergence of space. This creates a repulsive force that can destroy nearly any object it comes into contact with.

Hollow: Purple

Gojo's most powerful attack, created by combining Lapse: Blue and Reversal: Red. This technique neutralizes space itself, erasing anything it touches from existence. The concentration of power is so intense that even Gojo uses it sparingly.

DOMAIN EXPANSION: UNLIMITED VOID

Unlimited Void is Gojo's Domain Expansion, considered to be one of the most refined and powerful domains in existence. When activated, it forces targets to process an infinite amount of information in an instant, effectively paralyzing them and rendering them unable to act or defend themselves.

The domain appears as an infinite void filled with abstract geometric patterns. Anyone caught within it experiences everything in the universe simultaneously, overwhelming their senses completely. Only those with immense cursed energy or mental fortitude can withstand even a fraction of a second within this domain.

Six Eyes

Inherited Trait

The Six Eyes is a rare inherited trait of the Gojo clan that perfectly complements the Limitless technique. It grants Gojo extraordinary visual perception and cursed energy efficiency:

  • Enhanced Vision: Allows Gojo to see cursed energy in incredible detail, identifying its properties and weaknesses
  • Perfect Energy Control: Enables him to use his techniques with minimal cursed energy consumption
  • Automatic Analysis: Instantly analyzes and understands any technique used against him
  • Virtually Unlimited Stamina: The Six Eyes' efficiency means Gojo can maintain his techniques indefinitely

RELATIONSHIPS

Yuji Itadori

Gojo has taken a special interest in Yuji, seeing his potential as both a vessel for Sukuna and as a sorcerer. He fought to keep Yuji alive despite the jujutsu higher-ups wanting him executed, and often treats him with a playful, mentoring attitude.

Suguru Geto

Gojo and Geto were best friends during their time as students at Jujutsu High. Their relationship was complex and ultimately tragic, with Geto turning against jujutsu society and Gojo being forced to confront him.

Yuta Okkotsu

Gojo personally recruited Yuta and served as his mentor. He recognizes Yuta's immense potential as a special grade sorcerer and trusts him enough to entrust important missions to him.
