THE STORY OF JUJUTSU KAISEN

A world where curses born from human negativity lurk in the shadows

THE WORLD OF JUJUTSU KAISEN

In a world where negative emotions manifest as Curses, specially trained sorcerers fight to protect humanity. Follow the journey of Yuji Itadori as he enters the dangerous realm of Jujutsu sorcery.

ARC 1

CURSE ARC

Yuji Itadori is an unnaturally fit high school student living a normal life until he encounters his school's Occult Club in possession of a cursed object - a rotting finger. When the finger attracts dangerous curses to the school, Yuji swallows it to protect his friends, becoming the vessel for Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses.

Megumi Fushiguro, a young jujutsu sorcerer who was tracking the finger, witnesses Yuji's actions. Despite becoming possessed, Yuji maintains control of his body. This leads to a death sentence being postponed by Satoru Gojo, who convinces the jujutsu higher-ups to let Yuji live until he consumes all 20 of Sukuna's fingers, so the curse can be eliminated once and for all.

ARC 2

JUJUTSU HIGH ARC

Yuji enrolls at Tokyo Jujutsu High under Gojo's mentorship, where he meets fellow first-year students Nobara Kugisaki and Megumi Fushiguro. The trio begins learning about cursed energy and jujutsu techniques while completing missions to exorcise curses.

During one of their missions in a juvenile detention center, they encounter their first Special Grade curse, which tests their newly formed teamwork. Through these trials, the bonds between the first-year students grow stronger as they face the darker realities of the jujutsu world.

ARC 3

VS. MAHITO ARC

The first-year students face Mahito, a powerful and sadistic Special Grade curse who can manipulate souls with his Idle Transfiguration technique. During this confrontation, Yuji experiences profound loss when Junpei Yoshino, a troubled teen he befriended, is killed by Mahito.

This arc introduces the viewer to the main antagonistic curses — Mahito, Jogo, Hanami, and Choso — who are plotting something sinister under the guidance of a mysterious figure who resembles Suguru Geto, Gojo's former best friend turned curse user.

ARC 4

KYOTO SISTER SCHOOL EXCHANGE EVENT

Tokyo Jujutsu High students participate in a goodwill event with their sister school from Kyoto. What starts as a competitive tournament turns deadly when the antagonistic curses invade, seeking to eliminate Gojo and retrieve Sukuna's fingers.

During this arc, Yuji forms an unlikely friendship with Aoi Todo from Kyoto, who helps him better control his cursed energy. The students must set aside their rivalries to defend against the curse attack, showcasing their growth and abilities.

ARC 5

DEATH PAINTING ARC

Yuji and Nobara are assigned to investigate mysterious deaths, leading them to encounter the Death Paintings — half-human, half-curse brothers born from a woman and cursed spirit. The most powerful of these brothers, Choso, challenges Yuji in a fierce battle, believing him to be responsible for his siblings' deaths.

This arc explores the gray morality of the jujutsu world, as the Death Paintings, while dangerous, have human emotions and familial bonds. It also introduces important plot elements that become significant in later arcs.

ARC 6

GOJO'S PAST ARC

A flashback arc that explores Satoru Gojo and Suguru Geto's past as students at Jujutsu High. We see how they became known as the strongest duo and follow their mission to escort the Star Plasma Vessel, a girl named Riko Amanai who is central to a jujutsu tradition called Tengen's merger.

This arc reveals the tragic events that led to Geto's disillusionment with humanity and eventual turn to villainy, as well as how Gojo gained his infamous reputation as the strongest sorcerer. It provides crucial context for understanding the present-day conflicts and character motivations.

ARC 7

SHIBUYA INCIDENT ARC

On Halloween night, a complex plan orchestrated by curses unfolds in Shibuya. The antagonists seal Gojo within the Prison Realm, removing the strongest defense against curses. With Gojo trapped, jujutsu sorcerers must contain multiple threats across Shibuya while civilians are caught in the chaos.

This pivotal arc features numerous battles, significant character deaths, and major reveals that forever change the power balance in the jujutsu world. Yuji confronts Mahito in a definitive battle while dealing with the emotional weight of past trauma and current losses.

MOVIE

JUJUTSU KAISEN 0

A prequel story that follows Yuta Okkotsu, a timid high school student haunted by his childhood friend Rika, who became a powerful curse after her death. Unable to control Rika, Yuta is brought to Jujutsu High by Gojo, who recognizes his potential.

Under the guidance of Gojo and alongside classmates Maki Zenin, Toge Inumaki, and Panda, Yuta learns to control his cursed energy and Rika's power. The film culminates in a confrontation with Suguru Geto, who seeks to eliminate non-sorcerers and collect powerful cursed spirits.

ARC 8

CULLING GAME ARC

Following the Shibuya Incident, ancient sorcerers are reincarnated into modern vessels as part of a deadly battle royale called the Culling Game. Players must accumulate points by defeating other sorcerers or face death as penalty.

Yuji and his allies enter different colonies of the game with multiple objectives: to rescue Gojo from the Prison Realm, to save Megumi's sister Tsumiki who has become an unwilling player, and to negotiate with the mastermind behind the game to add rules that might save innocent lives.
