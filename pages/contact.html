FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I contribute content to the wiki?

You can contribute by submitting content through our contact form. Select "Content Suggestion" as the subject and provide details about what you'd like to add. Our editorial team will review your submission.

How do I report incorrect information?

Use our contact form with the subject "Correction Request" and provide the page URL along with the correct information. Our team will verify and update the content accordingly.

Do you accept fan art submissions?

Yes! We love featuring fan art. Please contact us with the subject "Content Suggestion" and include links to your artwork. Make sure you own the rights to any content you submit.

How can I join the wiki team?

We're always looking for passionate Jujutsu Kaisen fans to join our team. Send us a message with the subject "Collaboration Proposal" detailing your skills and experience with wikis or content creation.
