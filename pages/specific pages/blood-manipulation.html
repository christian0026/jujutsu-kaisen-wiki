BLOOD MANIPULATION

Choso
Innate Technique Semi-Grade 1

OVERVIEW

Choso’s Blood Manipulation Technique is a versatile inherited technique from the Kamo clan, allowing him to control his blood with high precision both inside and outside his body. As a Death Painting Womb with a modified body, Choso has exceptional control over blood flow and loss, enabling him to fight aggressively without weakening. He can manipulate the blood’s shape, density, and trajectory, creating ranged attacks like piercing blood bullets and reinforcing his body for close combat. His control also extends to clotting and rerouting blood to heal or resist injury.

One of his signature moves is Supernova, where he launches multiple high-speed blood orbs in all directions, overwhelming enemies with unpredictable attacks. Another is Blood Edge, forming hardened weapons from his blood for melee. Thanks to his unique biology, Choso can reabsorb spilled blood—something ordinary Blood Manipulation users can’t do—giving him both sustainability and lethality in battle. His technique balances deadly offense, defense, and self-repair, making him a formidable fighter even among Special Grade threats.

"You’re not taking anything else from my brothers."
— Choso

MECHANICS

Blood Manipulation technique revolve around controlling the properties and movement of blood using cursed energy. He can manipulate both the blood within his body and the blood he expels, altering its shape, density, speed, and even coagulation. By reinforcing his blood with cursed energy, he can turn it into hardened projectiles, weapons, or defensive tools.

Supernatural control of blood

A technique that uses cursed energy to freely control and weaponize blood for offense, defense, and support.

Blood Projectile Attacks

Choso can fire high-speed, pressurized blood bullets that pierce with extreme precision and force. He adjusts their speed, shape, and path mid-air for unpredictable trajectories.

Blood Hardening

By condensing cursed energy into blood, Choso can solidify it into sharp weapons or defensive tools like blades or shields. This gives him close-range combat utility beyond ranged attacks.

Blood Absorption

Unlike normal users, Choso can reabsorb his own spilled blood, preventing blood loss and regaining resources during a fight. This makes his technique sustainable in prolonged battles.

Internal Blood Control

Choso can manipulate his blood flow to avoid fatal damage, clot wounds, or redirect blood to vital areas. This grants him advanced durability and minor self-healing.

Multi-Directional AoE (Supernova)

With Supernova, Choso launches multiple blood orbs in all directions, each capable of homing in or exploding on contact. It overwhelms opponents by covering wide areas with simultaneous threats.

APPLICATIONS

Supernova (スーパーノヴァ, Sūpānova)

Choso releases multiple blood orbs in every direction, each capable of exploding on impact or homing in on enemies. This technique overwhelms opponents with rapid, multi-directional attacks that are difficult to avoid.

Multiple blood orbs in all directions, each of which can explode on contact with an enemy or object

These orbs can also home in on targets

Blood Edge (ブラッドエッジ, Buraddo Ejji)

By hardening his blood, Choso forms sharp, blade-like weapons that he can use for close combat. These weapons are highly durable and can be used to slice through enemies or deflect attacks.

Choso hardens his blood, turning it into sharp, blade-like weapon

The hardened blood blades provide both offensive and defensive capabilities

Piercing Blood Bullets (血液弾, Ketsueki-dan)

Choso forms condensed blood into high-speed projectiles that can pierce through targets with deadly precision. The blood bullets can be adjusted in trajectory and speed mid-flight for greater accuracy.

Turning his blood into deadly bullets

Piercing through enemies with extreme precision and force

Blood Shield (血の盾, Chi no Tate)

By hardening blood around him, Choso can create a shield to defend against attacks. This shield is strong enough to block most physical and cursed energy-based attacks, giving him protection during combat.

Choso hardens his blood to create a protective shield around him

Block both physical and cursed energy-based attacks

LIMITATIONS

Cursed Energy Consumption

Choso’s Blood Manipulation consumes a significant amount of cursed energy, especially for techniques like Supernova or Blood Edge. Prolonged use can drain his reserves, limiting his effectiveness in longer battles.

Specific Counters

Enemies who can rapidly regenerate, alter their body structure (like Mahito), or avoid direct physical contact can counter Choso’s attacks. Techniques that neutralize or disrupt cursed energy connections also prevent him from fully utilizing his blood-based abilities.

Domain Neutralization

While Choso does not have a Domain Expansion himself, he is vulnerable to enemy domains that can restrict movement or energy flow. Domains that warp space or sever external links can prevent his blood-based attacks from connecting or being effective.

Mental Strain

Manipulating his blood with precision requires constant focus and control, making Choso susceptible to mental fatigue during intense battles. The emotional toll of using his technique, especially when harming himself or others, can also affect his mental state.
